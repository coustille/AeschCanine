Serving the Aesch Community

We are here to serve dogs, dog owners, and animal lovers as well as our community.

Caring Staff

Our staff is comprised of local experts and volunteers who share an affection for dogs of all breeds and backgrounds.

Our Services:

  • Grooming
    • Keep your dog clean and healthy with a bath, brushing, fur clip, ear cleaning, nail trim, and teeth brushing.
  • Boarding
    • Give your dog a vacation while you are away. Allow them to socialize in a comfortable and safe environment with new friends.
  • Fostering
    • We provide a safe, loving environment with care and attention until for orphans until they find their forever home
  • Adoption
    • We work with local shelters, Petco, and Petsmart to place our fosters in permanent homes.
